About

Nowadays in India we can avail almost all essential services online like transportation,shopping,ordering food,consulting doctors etc, but if a common man faces any legal problems, unfortunately there is no digital platform to render the legal services.Therefore we want to build a website which brings common people and advocates all over the country into one platform, where anyone can render the services of the advocates by paying them online.The purpose of our website is to create awareness and making people fearless in pursuing legal actions, because majority of the people in india are afraid to proceed legally, as a result of this a lot of people are compromising with unfair settlements out of the law.we strongly believe that lack of legal knowledge and easy access to advocates are the root cause for prevailing injustice in the country.we hope this idea will be the saviour for the justice.
